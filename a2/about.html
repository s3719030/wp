What we do

Established in 2011, Harry Electricals has proudly helped many homes and businesses meet their electrical needs. As an Australian-owned company, we take pride in helping people save on electricity costs while helping the environemnt. As an accredited company, we are authorised to install new energy-efficient products in Victoria and New South Wales homes and businesses and create energy-efficiency certificates under both schemes.
